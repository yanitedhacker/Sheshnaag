# Code Of Conduct

Project Sheshnaag follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful, concrete, and safety-minded.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Keep technical criticism focused on code, evidence, and project goals.
- Respect maintainer decisions about defensive scope and release safety.
- Avoid publishing harmful operational details, secrets, or unauthorized target data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Attempts to turn the project into offensive campaign tooling.
- Publishing live exploit instructions, credentials, malware payloads, or third-party target details in project spaces.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer guidance on safety boundaries.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove unsafe content; close issues or PRs; and block contributors who violate these expectations.
